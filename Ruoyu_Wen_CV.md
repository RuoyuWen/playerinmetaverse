## Research Summary

My research and practice bridge theoretical inquiry and applied game development across cultural contexts, with a focus on **Human-Agent Interaction**, **Virtual Reality (VR)**, and **Alternate Reality Games (ARGs)**. I aim to design inclusive interactive systems that foster meaningful connection and cross-cultural understanding.

**Research Interests:** Human-Agent Interaction, Virtual and Extended Reality, Alternate Reality Games, Game Design, Player Psychology, Conversational AI, Cross-cultural HCI.

**Languages:** English; Chinese (Native).
