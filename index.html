Welcome

I'm Ruoyu Wen, a Game Researcher and Designer pursuing a PhD in Human Computer Interaction at the University of Canterbury, New Zealand. My research focuses on immersive entertainment experiences, particularly Alternate Reality Games (ARGs) and player motivation.

My work bridges theoretical research and practical game development, exploring how players engage with immersive digital experiences across different cultural contexts.
