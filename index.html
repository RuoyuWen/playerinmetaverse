About Me

I'm Ruoyu Wen, a PhD researcher specializing in Alternate Reality Games (ARGs) and player motivation analysis. My work focuses on understanding how players engage with immersive digital experiences using Self-Determination Theory frameworks.

My research bridges the gap between game design theory and practical application, investigating how player taxonomy and motivational landscapes shape engagement in ARGs and virtual environments. I'm passionate about the intersection of human psychology, game mechanics, and emerging technologies in the metaverse.
