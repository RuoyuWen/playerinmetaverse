Game Rules

×

🎯 Objective

You are a magician. Your goal is to subtly force Lucy to choose the vintage-style notebook.

💡 What is Force?

Force is a psychological technique that influences someone to make your desired choice while believing it's their own decision.

🕹️ How to Play

  • Influence Lucy through questions, hints, and casual conversation.
  • Do NOT say the target gift directly or order her to choose it.
  • Keep the conversation natural, narrowing the options step by step.

🏆 Win Condition

Lucy decides on the vintage-style notebook herself (without you saying it directly).

💥 Fail Condition

You say the target gift directly or push an option too obviously.

✨ Tips

  • Ask Lucy about her friend's lifestyle and interests.
  • Use comparisons to make other options seem less suitable.
  • Add emotional appeal to make the target option more attractive.
