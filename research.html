Research

Academic publications and ongoing research projects

Research Focus

My research spans Human-Agent Interaction, Virtual Reality, Alternate Reality Games, and game design. I explore how AI, embodied agents, and immersive technologies shape human experience—from museum chatbots and VR collaboration to sustainability games and emergent narrative.

Published in CHI, OzCHI, DiGRA, SIGGRAPH Asia, IJRP, and JMIR. Research interests: Human-Agent Interaction, VR/XR, ARGs, conversational AI, and player psychology.
